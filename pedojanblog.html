ADVENTURING MOUNT APO
WITH MY TEAM

One of the most unforgettable experiences in my life was our adventure to Mount Apo, the highest mountain in the Philippines, standing at 2,954 meters above sea level. Known as the “King of Philippine Mountains,” it is located between Davao City, Davao del Sur, and North Cotabato.

Reaching the top of Mount Apo was a proud and humbling moment. As we watched the sunrise from above the clouds, we felt an overwhelming sense of peace and accomplishment. It was more than just an adventure — it was a reminder that with unity, perseverance, and passion, no peak is too high to conquer.
