Top 5 Morning Exercises

Starting your morning with movement helps activate your metabolism, improve focus, and boost mood. Try a quick 15-minute routine: 10 squats, 10 push-ups, 30 seconds of planks, jumping jacks, and stretching. Even short workouts make a difference when done consistently.

Healthy Eating Tips

Fuel your body with the right balance of nutrients. Include more whole foods, vegetables, and lean proteins. Avoid processed sugar, and drink at least eight glasses of water daily. Small, steady dietary changes lead to long-term results and sustainable energy throughout the day.
